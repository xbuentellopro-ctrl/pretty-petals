Step 02

Tell us about
your order

After choosing your bouquet, you'll fill in a few quick details so we can make everything perfect.

1

Your Contact Info

Your name, email, and phone number. We'll use these to send your confirmation, payment link, and order updates.

2

Occasion & Date

Tell us what the flowers are for — a birthday, anniversary, Mother's Day, just because — and when you need them. We'll plan everything around your date.

3

Pickup or Delivery

Choose to pick up in Houston or have your arrangement delivered. If you select delivery, enter your address and we'll get it to you.

4

A Personal Touch

Add a card message, specify who the flowers are for, or leave any special notes. We read every single one.

No payment needed yet. Yazmin receives your request and reviews it personally.

Order confirmed — 50% deposit link sent

Once Yazmin confirms your order, you'll receive an email with a secure Square payment link for your deposit. This locks in your arrangement and date.

Yazmin creates your arrangement

We source fresh flowers and hand-craft your bouquet with care. You may receive updates along the way.

Order ready — final payment link sent

When your arrangement is complete, you'll receive a second payment link for the remaining balance. Pay it and your flowers are ready!

Your beautiful arrangement is delivered or ready for pickup in Houston. Enjoy!

We never store your card details. Every payment link goes directly through Square's secure checkout — the same platform used by millions of businesses worldwide. You'll see a Square checkout page when you click your link.
